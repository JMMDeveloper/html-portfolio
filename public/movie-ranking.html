James Morgan's Favorite Movies List

The Top 3 Best Movies According to James Morgan

Wicked (2024)

The music is masterfully composed and orchestrated, and it mixes lore from the original movie/book "The Wizard of Oz" with the adapted for stage book by Gregory McGuire entitled "Wicked: The Life and Times of the Wicked Witch of the West."

Also to mention are the performances of both Ariana Grande and Cynthia Erivo as Glinda and Elphaba. These women have an on-screen chemistry that's amazing - they really do look like best friends because they became that during filming! I've watched many interviews and behind-the-scenes featurettes for the movie, and I can tell that this film was definitely a passion project for all involved.

Avatar: The Way of Water (2022)

This movie was created by the masterful director James Cameron. He took his time creating it because in his opinion, "The technology didn't exist yet to do what the film required."

This sequel to the 2009 film Avatar was incredibly filmed, a large part of it underwater in stunning HD with beautiful alien sea life. It was one of the most visually stunning movies I've ever seen.

The Bourne Trilogy (2002, 2004, & 2007)

Yes, this is technically cheating as it's three movies, not one, but I couldn't pick only one from the series, so I'm naming it as one series.

This trilogy not only had pulse-pounding moment after moment, but was scored brilliantly by John Powell. My favorite thing about these films is Jason Bourne's intelligence, and how he's always a step ahead of the people tracking him. He's a brilliant mind, as well as an amazing martial artist.

He's the millenials' James Bond, without being overly flashy or relying on crazy gadgetry. One of my all-time favorite movie series. I re-watch it often!
