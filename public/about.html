About Me

Welcome to my corner of the internet! My name is James Morgan, and I’m passionate about creativity, innovation, and making a positive impact in the world. With a background in music, technology, and business, I’ve dedicated my life to exploring the harmony between art and science, creating meaningful experiences for myself and others.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from Berklee College of Music with a degree in Film Scoring and Electronic Production & Design, as well as a minor in Video Game Scoring. My journey began in vocal jazz and barbershop, where I developed a deep love for harmony and storytelling through music. Over the years, I’ve expanded my skills into areas like audio production, arranging, and scoring for multimedia projects. Whether I’m crafting a cappella arrangements or designing cinematic soundscapes, my goal is always the same: to create something that resonates deeply with people.

Beyond music, I’ve embraced my entrepreneurial spirit by founding multiple businesses, including Morgan Audio Design and Modern Vocals Online. These platforms allow me to merge my passion for teaching, creating, and sharing knowledge. From vocal learning tracks to film scores, I aim to inspire others to discover their unique voices and talents while building their confidence and skills.

When I’m not working, you’ll often find me diving into new interests, from building websites and exploring cutting-edge technologies to studying ways to achieve financial freedom. I love dreaming big and finding innovative solutions to challenges, always striving to turn ideas into reality. My ultimate mission is to create a life where creativity thrives, inspiring others to do the same. Thank you for visiting—let’s create something extraordinary together!
